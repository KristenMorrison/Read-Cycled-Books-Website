Our Story

In 2001, we started Read!cycled Books from our basement in Springfield, MO. We believe literature contributes to our society and well being. Everyone should have access to affordable books. That's where we come in! We sell gently used, fairly priced books from sellers all over the world. Shopping for secondhand books saves you money, is sustainable, and supports independent booksellers.

Millions of used copies for sale

Our online selection of used books covers every topic under the sun including all types of fiction and non-fiction.

  • Used paperbacks and hardcovers from all the major authors.
  • Beautiful vintage editions in memorable dust jackets.
  • Big books at cheap prices, from art books to coffee-table books
  • Thousands of used copies of romance novels.
  • Used self help books, from spiritual guidance to business books.
  • Mysteries, crime and adventure novels.

If you can't find what you are looking for, please fill out our Book Order form. We will be happy to locate a copy for you.
